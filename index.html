Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals to choose from below. Each one is worth a certain number of points. By clicking one, you add those points to your score.

If you can match the number of points you accumulate to the chosen number above the crystals, you win the game! Any more, and you'll just have to try again!

You don't know how many points a crystal is worth until you click it!

How many points a crystal is worth will change from game to game!

Good luck and have fun!
